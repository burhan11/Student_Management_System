About the Institute

Shri Govindram Seksaria Institute of Technology and Science,Indore was established in 1950.SGSITS is recognized as a leading institute in the state of Madhya Pradesh and the western region consistently during its glorious jouney of sixty five years .It has ranked among top 30-40 institutes of national level (including IITs and NITs ) by independent bodies.All India council for Technical Education(AICTE) and University Grant Commission (UGC) ,New Delhi ,has declared the institute as an autonomous in 1989.Under the autonomous state ,the institute is affiliated to the Rajiv Gandhi Prodhyogiki Vishwavidhyalaya (University of Technology of M.P.)Bhopal.

Presently ,the institute offers six UG courses(Regular) ,PG programmer with twenty two specialisation ,two part time PG courses in various discipline under the emerging areas of Engineering,Technology,Applied Sciences,Computer Applications.


Our Hostels

Apart from various spacious class rooms and well equipped laboratories,the campous of the institute encompasses four boys hostels ,two girls hostel,one transit hostel,and 44 faculty quarters.

Our Central Library


Institute has a Central Library Which has a vast collection of book ,reference library and a large number of subscribed online and print journals.
